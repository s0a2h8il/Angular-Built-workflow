Scheduled Workflows

You can trigger workflows on a schedule using POSIX cron syntax.

Syntax

on: schedule: # Runs at 5:30 UTC every day - cron: '30 5 * * *'

Cron Fields

┌───────────── minute (0 - 59)
│ ┌───────────── hour (0 - 23)
│ │ ┌───────────── day of the month (1 - 31)
│ │ │ ┌───────────── month (1 - 12 or JAN-DEC)
│ │ │ │ ┌───────────── day of the week (0 - 6 or SUN-SAT)
│ │ │ │ │
* * * * *

Every 15 minutes

*/15 * * * *

Every Monday at 1AM

0 1 * * 1
